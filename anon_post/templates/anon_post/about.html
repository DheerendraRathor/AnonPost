There are a lot of occasions when the students are unable to raise specific concerns with the authorities since they do not want their identity to be revealed.

Anonymous Post portal has been essentially made to allow these students post anonymously to the Dean Student Affairs.

However, while the portal is anonymous and no user details are stored in database, the user can be tracked on special requests.

Avoid any misuse of the facility, if found severe action will be taken.
